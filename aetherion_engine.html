Why Aetherion?

Blazing Fast Core

The core matching engine and risk services are built in **Rust**, offering unparalleled performance and memory safety for low-latency execution.

Massively Scalable Backend

Our cloud infrastructure, powered by **Go**, handles market data ingestion, order routing, and real-time data with extreme concurrency. Scale from ten to ten thousand strategies effortlessly.

Flexible & Modern Scripting

Utilize **Python** for rapid strategy development, backtesting, and data analysis. Our engine is built on a modern, standards-compliant toolchain for a stable and future-proof workflow.

A Polyglot Architecture That Performs
